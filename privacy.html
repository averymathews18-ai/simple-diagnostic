Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026

The short version: This is a simple website for a local mobile mechanic. It uses no cookies, no analytics, and no advertising trackers. We don't sell, rent, or trade your information — ever. The only information we get is what you choose to send us when you ask for service.

Simple Diagnostic Mobile Repair ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website. This page explains what information we collect through the site and how we handle it. We've kept it plain and honest. It is general information, not legal advice.

Information you provide

Our "Request service" form asks for your name and phone number and a description of the problem, and optionally your email, your city or ZIP, your vehicle, and when you need service. When you submit the form, your own device opens a pre-filled text message (or your phone dialer) addressed to us — you decide whether to send it. The form does not automatically transmit or store your details on a server. If you call or text us directly, we receive your phone number and messages the way any phone does.

How we use it

  • To respond to your service request and schedule the work
  • To contact you about your vehicle and the job
  • To keep ordinary records of work performed

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not send marketing messages unless you separately ask us to.

Cookies, analytics, and tracking

This website sets no cookies and runs no analytics, advertising pixels, or cross-site tracking tools.

"Do Not Track" signals

Because we do not track visitors across websites or over time, we do not respond differently to a browser's "Do Not Track" setting. We do not permit third parties to collect personally identifiable information about your activity across other websites through this site.

Sharing your information

We do not sell or share your personal information for anyone else's marketing. We share information only when it's needed to do the work you asked for — for example, ordering a part for your vehicle — or when the law requires it.

Text messages and phone calls

If you give us your phone number, you are asking us to contact you by phone or text about your request. Message and data rates from your carrier may apply. You can ask us to stop contacting you at any time and we will.

Children's privacy

This site is meant for adults arranging vehicle repair and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

Keeping your information safe

We keep your information only as long as needed to serve you and meet ordinary record-keeping obligations, and we take reasonable steps to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You can ask what information we have about you, ask us to correct or delete it, or ask us to stop contacting you — just reach out using the contact details below. We honor these requests regardless of where you live, including California residents.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your information, we'll update this page and change the effective date at the top.
