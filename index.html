Common questions

Straight answers, before you call.

Do you actually come to me? +
Yes — that's the whole idea. We bring the shop to your driveway, your workplace parking lot, or the side of the road. No tow truck, no waiting room. Just tell us where you are and what the vehicle is doing.
What areas do you cover? +
The Gulf Coast from Fort Myers up to Sarasota — including Cape Coral, North Fort Myers, Punta Gorda, Port Charlotte, North Port, Englewood and Venice, plus everywhere in between. Broke down while traveling through? We help visitors too.
What are your hours? +
We're open seven days a week, from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Call or text (941) 273-5751 and we'll get to you as fast as we can — often the same day.
What do you work on? +
Cars, trucks and trailers. Check-engine and computer diagnostics, alternators and starters, batteries, brakes, A/C, cooling and overheating, belts and hoses, sensors and warning lights, tune-ups, oil changes, trailer lights, wiring, bearings and axles, and emergency roadside repair.
Can everything be fixed on the spot? +
Most of it. We carry the same diagnostic tools and know-how as a shop, so the majority of jobs get finished right where your vehicle sits. If something genuinely needs a lift, we'll tell you straight rather than run up a bill. Not sure about your situation? Call and ask.
How do I know what it will cost? +
You hear a fair price up front, before any work starts. We fix what's actually broken — nothing you don't need, and no surprise line items at the end.
What payment do you accept? +
Cash, credit and debit.
